Attempts to tamper, suppress, interfere, or modify the platform or its record structures constitute unlawful obstruction of:

  • Public interest research (GDPR Art. 89)
  • Evidence gathering for legal claims (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f), 9(2)(f))

Any such conduct is reportable and actionable under the:

  • Computer Misuse Act 1990
  • Fraud Act 2006
  • Data Protection Act 2018 – Schedule 2, Part 5
  • Criminal Justice and Courts Act 2015 – Section 17
